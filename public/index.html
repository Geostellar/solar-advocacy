Generate power with a click
Getting solar was a great idea, right? It is cost effective - and good for the environment. And great ideas deserve to be shared. Share your solar story on Facebook and help multiply solar power in your community.
How can sharing on Facebook make an impact? Well, we’ve done the math.
You are a solar owner and you have 150 friends (U.S. average), who also have 150 friends. That’s 22,500 friends. So imagine if every U.S. solar owner shared their story. That would be 140,000,000 more people who know the benefits of solar. That is a whole lot of power for just one click.
And even better, research shows that people who know somebody with solar, make the choice to go solar themselves 3x quicker. So by sharing your story on Facebook, you’ll be helping to build a huge solar community - faster.
